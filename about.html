Biography

I am Kanishkar V, an enthusiastic Electronics and Communication Engineer currently pursuing my degree at Karpagam Institute of Technology in Coimbatore. With a strong foundation in electronics and a keen interest in technology, I have developed a passion for creating innovative solutions that leverage the power of electronics and software.

My journey in the field of electronics began in high school, where I was fascinated by how electronic devices work and how they can be used to solve real-world problems. This curiosity led me to pursue a degree in Electronics and Communication, where I have gained hands-on experience in various projects, including PCB design, troubleshooting, and software development.

I have a solid understanding of programming languages such as Python, Java, and C++, which I have applied in various projects, including developing machine learning models and creating applications that enhance user experience. My recent projects, such as the Customized Article Delivery with AI and Fake News Detection, showcase my ability to integrate technology with practical applications.

In addition to my technical skills, I am a strong advocate for continuous learning. I have completed several certifications, including Python Basics from HackerRank and AWS Academy Cloud Foundations, which have equipped me with the knowledge to tackle complex challenges in the tech industry.

Outside of academics, I enjoy participating in hackathons and tech meetups, where I can collaborate with like-minded individuals and share ideas. I believe that teamwork and collaboration are essential for innovation, and I am always eager to learn from others.

Looking ahead, I aspire to work in a dynamic environment where I can contribute to cutting-edge projects and continue to grow as a professional. I am particularly interested in areas such as artificial intelligence, IoT, and embedded systems, and I am excited about the potential they hold for transforming industries and improving lives.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio! I look forward to connecting with you and exploring opportunities to collaborate on exciting projects.
